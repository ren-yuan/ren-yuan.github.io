Ren Yuan

Human = ?

Immersive Video, 4/5 Screens, Variable, 4'30", 2015-Present (2023 Version)


Human = ? is an immersive video work created entirely with computer programming, combining thoughts on philosophy, science and art. The work presents the process of human understanding of the world, oneself and existence through the artist's subjective perspective in an abstract and semi-narrative way. This work will be continuously updated as the exploration progresses.
